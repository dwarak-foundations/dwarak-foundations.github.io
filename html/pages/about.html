About Us


Mr.R.Varadharajan

Designated Partner Dwarak Foundations

Dwarak Foundations Chennai LLP started out as a small construction company by Mr.R. Varadharajan in the year 1998. Now, we take great pride in announcing that we have completed around 50+ projects which include #8 Villas, #18 Duplex houses, #27 Apartment projects all over Chennai. We trust that our company grows only when our clients are satisfied to the fullest. By providing more value for the money and a higher standard of living, we are able to make our clients happy which in return brings more prospective clients. One common question that new clients and friends ask is, why haven’t we invested in marketing or publicizing? For them we say, we have been investing in marketing and publicizing since day 1 by making our clients happy. Our clients are our only marketing people and reviewers who spread words about our first-class quality products and customer service. Please check out what our clients have to say about us.



Dwarak Foundations Chennai LLP is a construction company based in Chennai.
